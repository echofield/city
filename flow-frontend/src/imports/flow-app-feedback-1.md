Why glyphs work better for Flow

Drivers scan extremely fast. Glyphs can become recognition patterns.

Example mental mapping after a few days:

↗ transport exit
◉ zone pressure
△ event cluster
▣ venue hub

Drivers won’t read the label anymore.
They’ll just recognize the pattern.

This is exactly how Bloomberg terminals and trading dashboards work.
